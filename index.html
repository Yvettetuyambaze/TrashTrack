About us

Built for Rwanda’s waste collection reality

Many households do not use smartphones. TrashTrack reaches them by SMS while offering maps and dashboards for those who pay for premium service.

Our mission

Reduce missed pickups, improve payment tracking, and help collection companies run efficient routes with full transparency.

Our services

What we offer

End-to-end tools for modern waste collection operations in urban Rwanda.

SMS alerts

Night-before reminders, in-the-area pings, and missed pickup notices — no smartphone required.

Live maps

Drivers follow optimised routes; paying clients see the truck approaching in real time.

Payments & admin

Track MoMo and cash, update customer status, and manage weekly collection schedules.

Household portal

Clients view notifications and premium map features from any phone browser.

Driver app

Operations dashboard

Plans & pricing

Plans for every role

Collection companies subscribe by plan. SMS reminders are billed per message on each household's monthly bill — your platform fee is separate.

All prices shown in USD ($)

Household · Client

Driver

Company · Admin

Staff roles (CEO, COO, HR, Finance, Auditor, IT), client registration, contracts with QR, GIS audit map, and MoMo auto-pay — scale from 150 to unlimited households.
